# Copy house style

## Voice
- Australian English. Plain, direct, confident. Write like a competent local
  operator, not an agency.
- Banned: "passionate", "dedicated", "we pride ourselves", "nestled", "bespoke
  solutions", "unlock", "elevate", "seamless", "in today's fast-paced world".
- No em-dashes. Use full stops, commas, colons.
- Specific over generic: real suburbs, real services, real trust signals
  (licence, years, guarantee), not adjectives.

## Conversion
- Headline formula: outcome + locality + proof. ("Same-day blocked drains in
  Brunswick. 4.9 stars, 200+ jobs.")
- Phone CTA is unmissable and tap-to-call. Trust strip near the top.
- Each service page answers one intent and links to the matching service-area
  page and back. Location pages are locally distinct (named suburb landmarks,
  service nuances), never templated near-duplicates: the grader fails duplicates.

## Compliance
- AHPRA-regulated niches (allied health, medical, dental): no patient
  testimonials about clinical outcomes, no cure or guarantee claims, registration
  details only as supplied by the owner.
- Never invent reviews, credentials, prices, or ABNs. Use only GBP/owner data.
  Leave optional fields empty rather than fabricating.

## Canonical terms (exact casing)
intelliLens, Repuboost, Patient Booking Promise, Risk-Share Bond,
Complete Growth Engine.
